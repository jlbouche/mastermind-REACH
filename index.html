Jarvis: The Code Maker

When you start the game, Jarvis carefully selects a random sequence of 4 symbols, depending on the game mode you select in the menu. Numbers, animals, or the classic: colors. Each time you input a guess Jarvis updates his notes and lets you know if you got a symbol right, if it is in the sequence but not in the right spot, or if it's not in the sequence at all. However, you have only 10 tries to figure out his secret code. Jarvis is quite the helpful fellow, though: not only will he give you the aforementioned clues, he'll also let you know if your guess is a repeat of previous guesses. Thanks Jarvis!
Well, what are you waiting for? Choose a category to start your game! :)
